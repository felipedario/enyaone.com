Is this really an Enya song?

It's worth noting that there are certain songs that are occasionally mistakenly attributed to Enya. For example, "Harry's Game" is actually performed by Clannad, while "Adiemus" is the work of Karl Jenkins. Additionally, there are instances where people might mistakenly believe they have heard Enya's voice on soundtracks like Gladiator or Titanic.
