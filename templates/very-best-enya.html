The Very Best of Enya (2009)

Enya's latest greatest hits album, The Very Best of Enya, graced the music scene on November 20th 2009, capturing the essence of her illustrious career in one extraordinary collection. This remarkable album offers a comprehensive journey through Enya's expansive discography, meticulously curated by the artist herself, alongside the creative input of Nicky Ryan and Roma Ryan. Spanning across Enya's diverse albums, this collection showcases her most cherished and iconic songs, carefully selected to provide a captivating musical experience for both devoted fans and newcomers alike.

For those seeking an even deeper immersion into Enya's enchanting world, a special edition of "The Very Best of Enya" includes a bonus DVD featuring mesmerizing documentary footage. This exclusive addition offers an intimate glimpse into Enya's creative process, providing a captivating behind-the-scenes exploration of her artistic vision and the collaborative efforts that have shaped her musical legacy.

The unofficial announcement of this new collection, twelve years after the first, Paint the Sky with Stars - The Best of Enya (1997), appeared on August 15, 2009 on the albumvote.co.uk website, which had also communicated the publication for November 16th. The news was then confirmed on August 26 on the official website. On September 19 on the official forum Unity, Roma Ryan released the cover of the album. The CD-only edition contains 18 tracks while the CD/DVD edition has 22 tracks and 13 videos plus 3 documentaries. To these two editions a third will be added: the Collector's Edition; of it — which can be purchased mainly on the Enya online store and other specialized sites — only 1.000 copies were printed, of which 300 reserved for the Japanese market only (as indicated on the Japanese site Warner Music Direct).
