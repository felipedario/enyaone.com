Even In The Shadows, 2015

About

Released in November 2015, this is the video for Enya song "Even In The Shadows", the fourth from her new album Dark Sky Island.

Time: 4:14

"Even in the Shadows" features a double bass played by Irish rock and jazz musician Eddie Lee. Lee was a member of Those Nervous Animals from Sligo, Ireland who were label mates, under the Tara Music label, of the Brennan family band Clannad in the 1980s; Enya was a member of Clannad from 1980 to 1982.
